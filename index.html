Role of Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) Owners Associations of Bangladesh

  • Creating a vibrant SME sector for economic development of the country.
  • Co-operating the entrepreneurs in financing, skill development, overcoming marketing obstructions with discussion to Government agencies like NBR, Controller of Exports of Imports, Bangladesh Bank etc.
  • Providing support to the entrepreneurs for importing and exporting of SME goods and services that listed by the central bank.
  • To take other necessary measures for development of SME industries by discussions with the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, FBCCI and SME Foundation.
  • Creating awareness by organizing seminars, symposiums, training programs for those engaged in SME sectors.
